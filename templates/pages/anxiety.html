Anxiety

Anxiety is a natural response to stress, but when it becomes chronic, it can interfere with daily life. Anxiety disorders can affect relationships, work, and health.

Symptoms

  • Excessive worrying
  • Restlessness
  • Increased heart rate
  • Difficulty sleeping
  • Panic attacks

Prevention

While anxiety can't always be prevented, you can reduce its impact with:

  • Regular physical exercise
  • Practicing mindfulness and meditation
  • Maintaining a healthy diet
  • Seeking support from loved ones
