Suicidal Thoughts

Suicidal thoughts are signs of severe emotional distress. These thoughts need immediate attention and professional help.

Symptoms

  • Talking about feeling hopeless
  • Withdrawing from loved ones
  • Sudden mood swings
  • Engaging in risky behavior

Prevention

Prevention includes:

  • Encouraging open communication
  • Providing emotional support
  • Accessing crisis services
