Stress

Stress is the body's reaction to any change that requires an adjustment or response. It can be both physical and emotional.

Symptoms

  • Headaches or muscle tension
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty concentrating
  • Changes in sleep patterns

Prevention

Managing stress can include:

  • Practicing time management
  • Engaging in hobbies
  • Avoiding unhealthy coping mechanisms
