About Mental Health

Mental health includes our emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Mental health problems are common, but support is available. Here are some of the common mental health conditions:

Anxiety

Anxiety involves intense, excessive, and persistent worry or fear about everyday situations.

Learn more

Depression

Depression is a mood disorder that causes persistent feelings of sadness and loss of interest.

Learn more

Stress

Stress is a feeling of emotional or physical tension that can come from any event or thought that makes you feel frustrated, angry, or nervous.

Learn more

Suicidal Thoughts

Suicidal thoughts involve thinking about or planning suicide. It's a sign of mental distress and requires immediate attention.
